A SUSHI RECIPE

FOR YOURSELF

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice: 2 cups
  • Nori Sheets: 5-6 sheets
  • Fresh Salmon: 200g sliced
  • Avocado: 1 ripe
  • Cucumber: 1 medium
  • Rice Vinegar: ¼ cup
  • Sugar: 2 tbsp

Preparation

1. Rinse sushi rice until water runs clear. Cook as per instructions.

2. Mix vinegar, sugar, and salt. Fold into warm rice.

3. Place nori on mat, spread rice, add fillings, and roll tightly.

4. Slice with wet knife and serve with soy sauce.
